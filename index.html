Listing your property is easy! Click the "List Your Home" button, fill out the required details, upload high-quality photos, and submit. Your property will be live shortly.

No, listing your property is completely free. We only charge a small service fee if you successfully connect with a buyer.

All buyers are verified through a strict process, including identity checks and financial capability assessments, ensuring only serious buyers contact you.

It depends on the demand in your area. However, most users connect with potential buyers within 48 hours of listing.
